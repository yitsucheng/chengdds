Permanent Dental Bridge Dentist Chamblee GA

A bridge, also called a “fixed bridge” or a “fixed dental prosthesis,” is a restoration that replaces one or more missing teeth. It extends across an area that has no, or missing teeth and is made up of an artificial tooth fused between 2 crowns. The bridge is held firmly in place by your own teeth on each side of the missing teeth, or by dental implants. A bridge is permanent and cannot be removed.

What Are the Benefits of Dental Bridges?

  • Restore your smile
  • Restore the ability to properly chew and speak
  • Prevent remaining teeth from drifting, or shifting.
  • Maintain the shape of your face.
  • Distribute the forces in your bite properly by replacing missing teeth
