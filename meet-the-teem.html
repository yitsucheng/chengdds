Meet The Team

  • Dr. Yi Tsu Cheng
    Dentist

    Dr. Yi Tsu Cheng was born in Taipei, Taiwan. She received her Bachelor of Medicine in Dentistry degree from Taipei Medical College in Taipei, Taiwan. Following her graduation, she completed a hospital-based residency at Chang Gung Memorial hospital.After moving to United States, Dr. Cheng received her Doctor of Dental Surgery (DDS) degree from Baylor College of Dentistry, now a Texas A & M University System Health Science Center.

    During her years in dental school, Dr. Cheng attended special training in pediatric dentistry at Scottish Rite Children’s Hospital in Dallas, Texas. She graduated with prestigious Dean’s Honor.
    Dr. Cheng is a fellow member of Georgia Dental Association, Texas Dental Association, Academy of General Dentist, American Academy of Esthetic Dentistry and Chinese Medical Association. She has been practicing dentistry in Taiwan and Atlanta for over 25 years.

    Dr. Cheng has completed extensive general and cosmetic dentist trainings in the fields of:

    • Botox & Dermal Filler for TMJ and Bruxism
    • Implant Dentistry
    • Invisalign
    • Endodontic Treatment
    • Periodontal Surgery
    • Prosthetics

  • Dr. Hosam Abochhab
    Dentist

    Dr. Hosam Abochhab (Abo) received his Doctor of Dental Surgery (DDS) from University of Damascus in Syria. After moving to the United States, Dr. Abochhab continued his dentistry education and received his Doctor of Dental Medicine (DMD) at the University of Pennsylvania, an Ivy League school in 2006. Dr. Abochhab practiced dentistry in Pennsylvania and Ohio for several years before moving to Georgia in 2008.

    Dr. Abochhab is dedicated to his patient’s complete dental care and committed to provide the highest levels of preventive, restorative, and aesthetic care. He is a member of the American Dental Association, Academy of General Dentistry, and Georgia Academy of Cosmetic Dentistry. Dr. Abochhab continues to further advance his skills through ongoing educational dental courses. His goal is to enhance his patients’ quality of their dental needs.

    Dr. Abochhab enjoys all aspects of dentistry, specifically oral surgery and cosmetic dentistry.

  • Dr. Vy Do
    D.M.D.

    Dr. Vy Do was born in Saigon, Vietnam and moved to the states when he was 4. He has been in Georgia ever since and currently resides in the greater Atlanta area.

    After finishing high school as the class valedictorian, he attended the University of Georgia on a full academic scholarship. During his tenure at UGA, Dr. Do was active in many school-wide and community-related activities, including the American Chemical Society, Vietnamese Student Association, UGA Symphony Orchestra, and the Athens Symphony Orchestra. As part of the UGA Honors program, he spent one summer abroad in Italy, where he took courses in genetics and art history, focusing on Italian Renaissance art. He culminated his studies by graduating Summa Cum Laude with Honors with his bachelor of science in biology and was also inducted in the Phi Kappa Phi Honors Society. He maintains ties with UGA by serving the office of admissions as a liaison.

    Afterwards, Dr. Do attended the Medical College of Georgia (now known as Augusta University) to obtain his Doctor of Dental Medicine degree. He was again active in many activities in school and around the community including giving violin lessons at the Jessye Norman School of the Arts and being a liaison for the International Student Exchange Program. Upon graduation, Dr. Do was inducted into the Alpha Upsilon Phi Honors Society for his leadership and community contributions.

    Dr. Do immediately went into private practice and came to Dr. Cheng’s office in June 2015. He maintains a busy working schedule and takes continuing education courses to stay up-to-date with modern advances in dentistry. His main interests include cosmetic dentistry, oral medicine, and oral pathology.

    In his spare time, Dr. Do enjoys playing tennis (he is a huge fan of Rafael Nadal) and cooking. He is also an avid runner, participating in several half marathons and his first marathon in 2014 in Chicago. Furthermore, he is a self-proclaimed “foodie”, lover of fiction and classical musician. He currently plays viola with the Gwinnett Symphony Orchestra. But most of all, Dr. Do lives for international travel and tries to visit new foreign countries as often as possible.
    Additionally, Dr. Do is the youngest of 5 and uncle of 11.
